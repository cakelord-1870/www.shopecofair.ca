Holiday Series Quote Tin

$10.00 $20.00

These popular fair trade travel tin candles are back with holiday scents and inspiring quotes. These fragrances conjure the warmth of the holidays!

A great hostess gift and perfect way to be inspired during the holiday season, wherever you go.

Peppermint Drop
"Giving opens the way to receiving."   Florence Scovel Shinn

Chai Tea
"Optimism is the faith that leads to achievement."  Helen Keller

Cinnamon & Clove
"Life itself is the proper binge."  Julia Child

Blue Fir & Spice
"The best thing to hold onto in life is each other."  Audrey Hepburn

Vanilla & Cream
"Spread love wherever you go." Mother Teresa

Wax:  soy blend candle with cotton wick
Fragrance:  made with essential oils
Dimensions:  4oz is 3” W x 2” H
Burn time:  4oz about 18 hours
Container:  recyclable aluminum 
Packaging: story cards of the women candle-makers included

Prosperity Candle is a social enterprise that supports refugees and artisans through candle-making and beautifully crafted vessels easily refilled or repurposed for a lifetime of enjoyment.

Every candle is handpoured by a woman artisan building a brighter future for herself and her family using the highest quality waxes, natural cotton and wood wicks, and premium fragrances made with essential oils. They never add any dyes or enhancers. 

WFTO associate member.
