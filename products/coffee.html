Coffee

$4.00 $12.00

    

Spirit Bear Coffee Company is an Indigenous Canadian coffee company sourcing and craft-roasting some of the finest organic and fair-trade coffee in the world. At coffee origins in South America and Asia they support the health of farmers through organic farming methods and ensure that they are paid fairly via fair-trade premiums.

Spirit Bear Coffee Company is also teaming up with Ocean Alliance and the Drones For Whale Research program SnotBot®, a drone that collects a priceless biological data set from the whales exhalation, a non-instrusive way to assess the health of the whale which mirrors the health of our oceans. They will donate $1 for every 400 g bag and $2 for every 1 kg bag sold. Buy coffee, save whales. More info   www.spiritbearcoffeecompany.com

Available in 70 g, 400 g and 1 kg bags, or 70 g in a beautiful Nepali shoulder bag!
